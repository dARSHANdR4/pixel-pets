## 🐾 Pixel Pets

**Your adorable pixel companion that lives on every webpage.**  
A fully-featured Chrome Extension built with Vanilla JS, Canvas 2D, and a Neo-Brutalism design system.

![Pixel Pets Banner](assets/banner.png)

Pixel Pets brings a tiny animated pet to every website you visit. Feed it, play with it, and unlock achievements to reveal secret pet species!

### ✨ Features

- 🐾 **10 pet types** — Classic Cat, Black Cat, Puppy, Bunny, Fox, Ghost & more; unlock Hamster, Dragon via achievements
- 🖱️ **Cursor-following** with idle, walk, and run animations
- 💙 **Mood & energy system** with feed/play/sleep interactions and particle effects
- 🏆 **10 achievements** across Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers
- ⚙️ **Full settings page** — size, speed, personality, multi-pet support (up to 5 simultaneous)
- 🔒 **100% local** — zero data sent to any server, zero tracking
- 🎨 **Neo-Brutalism design** — bold, pixel-art-inspired UI
