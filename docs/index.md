## Pixel Pets

**A virtual pixel-art companion that lives on your browser.**

![Pixel Pets Banner](assets/banner.png)

Pixel Pets brings a tiny animated pet to every website you visit. Feed it, play with it, and unlock achievements to reveal secret pet species!

### ✨ Features
- 10 pet types — unlock Hamster, Dragon & Ghost via achievements
- Cursor-following with idle, walk, and run animations
- Mood & energy system with feed/play/sleep interactions
- 10 achievements across Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers
- Full settings page — size, speed, personality, multi-pet support
- 100% local — zero data sent to any server
