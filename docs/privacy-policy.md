# Privacy Policy for Pixel Pets

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

---

## Overview

Pixel Pets ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that renders a virtual pixel-art pet companion on web pages you visit. This Privacy Policy describes how the Extension handles your information.

---

## Information We Collect

**Pixel Pets does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information.**

All data generated by the Extension is stored exclusively on your local device using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The Extension stores the following data **only on your device**:

| Data Type | Purpose | Where Stored |
|---|---|---|
| Pet configuration (name, type, personality) | Saves your pet customization | `chrome.storage.local` |
| Pet stats (mood, energy, play counts) | Powers the pet interaction system | `chrome.storage.local` |
| Achievement progress | Tracks your in-game achievements | `chrome.storage.local` |
| Extension settings (size, speed, opacity) | Remembers your preferences | `chrome.storage.local` |
| Unlocked pet accessories | Tracks earned rewards | `chrome.storage.local` |

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Pixel Pets does **not**:

- Collect, store, or transmit your browsing history
- Access, read, or transmit any content from websites you visit
- Collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, location, etc.)
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Send any data to external servers or third parties
- Use analytics or crash reporting services
- Share any data with advertisers or data brokers

---

## Permissions Used

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Saves pet data, settings, and achievements locally on your device |
| `alarms` | Runs periodic pet mood decay and auto-save timers |
| `host_permissions: <all_urls>` | Required to render the pet overlay on web pages you visit. The Extension **reads no content** from these pages. |

---

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all Extension data at any time by:

1. Opening the Extension Settings page
2. Going to **Advanced** tab
3. Clicking **Reset All Data**

You can also uninstall the Extension at any time via `chrome://extensions`, which removes all associated local data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Pixel Pets does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. As no personal data is collected by the Extension, it is safe for users of all ages.
